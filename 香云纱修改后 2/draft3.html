Introduce

Xiangyun Sha, or "Fragrant Cloud Silk," is a traditional Chinese silk fabric renowned for its smooth texture, softness, and unique luster. Its production involves several intricate steps: selecting high-quality mulberry silk, soaking it in natural plant extracts, sun-drying, dyeing with natural plant dyes, polishing with river stones, steaming, washing, and finally air-drying and shaping. This process ensures the fabric's exceptional quality, characterized by even, long-lasting colors and a unique sheen. Xiangyun Sha is breathable, environmentally friendly, and highly durable, making it a luxurious and culturally significant textile that continues to be valued in modern fashion.
